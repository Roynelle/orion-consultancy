About ORION

A trusted provider of professional security and consultancy services dedicated to safeguarding operations and supporting strategic growth.

Who We Are

Founded in 2012, ORION Consultancy Incorporated (OCI) is a duly registered and accredited security and consultancy firm recognized by the Liberia Maritime Authority (LiMA) and the Ministry of Justice (MOJ).

OCI was established to provide high-quality professional services to the growing market of corporate and industrial clients across Liberia. Since inception, we have successfully delivered a wide range of security and consultancy solutions to dozens of respected institutions and private clients.

As our client portfolio continues to expand, we remain committed to refining our expertise, strengthening operational discipline, and mastering the art of client satisfaction.

Our growth has never compromised our standards. We continue to uphold excellence, integrity, and consistency in delivering high-quality services nationwide.

Our Mission
